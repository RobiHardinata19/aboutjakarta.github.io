Jakarta

Jakarta, or officially known as the Special Capital Region of Jakarta or Greater Jakarta, is the nation's capital and largest city in Indonesia. Jakarta is the only city in Indonesia that has a provincial-level status.

Jakarta with a million dreams

Millions of people in this city are risking their fate along every road point just to hope that they can change their lives and not surrender to fate. The streets that are passed at every point of the city are silent witnesses of how hard they bet that the dream must come true.

Even though in the end they just surrendered to fate, at least they are with millions of people who have the same dream, and Jakarta is still a million dreams for those who want to fight for it.

The Other Side of Jakarta

Behind the glittering city and tall skyscrapers, there is another side of Jakarta which has been a problem for a long time, namely poverty, social inequality, and slums that adorn the other side of life in the capital city.
